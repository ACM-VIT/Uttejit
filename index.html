About VIT

VIT was established with the aim of providing quality higher education on par with international standards. It persistently seeks and adopts innovative methods to improve the quality of higher education. The campus has a cosmopolitan atmosphere with students from all corners of the globe.. The global standards set at VIT in the field of teaching and research, spur us on in our relentless pursuit of excellence. The highly motivated youngsters on the campus are a constant source of pride. Many of our students, who pursue their research projects in foreign universities, bring high quality to their work and esteem to India and have done us proud. With steady steps, we continue our march forward.
